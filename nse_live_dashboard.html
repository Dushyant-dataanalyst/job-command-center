F&O Strategy Framework
↑ Market BULLISH
• BUY ATM Call (CE) — strong trend, ADX>25
• Bull Call Spread — moderate trend, cap cost
• Cash-secured Put Sell — want stock at lower price
↓ Market BEARISH
• BUY ATM Put (PE) — strong downtrend confirmed
• Bear Put Spread — limit premium cost
• Covered Call Sell — on holdings to earn premium
→ Market SIDEWAYS
• Sell ATM Straddle — collect CE+PE premium
• Iron Condor — hedge wings, defined max loss
• Calendar Spread — time decay play
⚡ Key F&O Rules
• Never hold options overnight (theta decay)
• Max loss = premium paid (for buyers)
• Square off by 3:15 PM — avoid expiry risk
• Start with index options, not stock options
